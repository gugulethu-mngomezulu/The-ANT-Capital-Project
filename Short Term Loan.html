Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much can I borrow with The Ant Capital's Short-Term Personal Loan?
You can borrow any amount up to R7,000, depending on your affordability and credit profile.
2. What do I need to apply for a personal loan?
To apply, simply provide:

• A copy of your ID
• 3 most recent payslips
• 3 months’ latest bank statements

Your application is processed and approved on the same day, and the money is instantly transferred to your account once approved.

Yes, we conduct credit checks and assess your affordability to ensure responsible lending.

No, The Ant Capital does not offer loan consolidation services. Our focus is on short-term personal loans only.
