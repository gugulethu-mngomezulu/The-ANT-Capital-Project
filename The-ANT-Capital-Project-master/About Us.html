About The Ant Capital

Founded in 2020, The Ant Capital is a youth-driven alternative finance company, revolutionizing the government tendering industry in KwaZulu-Natal and the Eastern Cape province. Our primary focus is purchase order funding, offering fast, efficient, and accessible funding solutions for small, medium, and micro enterprises (SMMEs).

Our Culture

We are a youth-driven organisation that strives for inclusivity, constant innovation and the development of our people and communities around us guided by our values.

Our North Star

Our vision is to realise an environment where SMMEs have equal access to government tendering opportunities without being restricted by lack of financial resources.

Our Journey

In 2020, two budding entrepreneurs in Kokstad, KwaZulu-Natal came together with a vision: to bridge the financial gap that prevented many small businesses from thriving. They saw talented entrepreneurs winning government purchase orders but struggling to deliver because of a lack of funding. Determined to change this, they founded The Ant Capital, setting out to create equal access to government procurement opportunities for SMMEs.

Building a Foundation for Growth

By 2021, The Ant Capital officially began operations, providing fast and innovative purchase order funding that allowed SMMEs to deliver on their contracts with ease. The company quickly became a trusted financial partner, helping businesses unlock opportunities and drive economic growth in underserved communities. Recognising the need for accessibility, The Ant Capital opened its first office in 2022, right in the heart of Kokstad at the Rolyats Regional Centre. This move made it easier for SMMEs to reach us, ensuring that entrepreneurs had direct access to the financial solutions they needed to grow.

Expanding Our Impact

In 2023, The Ant Capital took a major step forward. After securing its Financial Services Provider (FSP) and National Credit Regulator (NCR) certifications, the company expanded its offerings to include short-term personal loans. This addition wasn’t just about growing the business—it was about improving the financial well-being of individuals, giving them access to fast, reliable credit when they needed it most.
