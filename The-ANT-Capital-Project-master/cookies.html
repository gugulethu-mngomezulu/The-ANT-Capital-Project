Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 08 May 2025 Last Updated: 08 May 2027

1. Use of Cookies

The Ant Capital uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, deliver targeted advertising, and analyze website traffic.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Necessary for website functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies – Help us understand how users interact with the website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functionality Cookies – Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Advertising Cookies – Deliver relevant ads based on your browsing behavior.

Our website serves as an online application portal for these services and a platform for collecting information for marketing and communication purposes.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from:

  • Google Analytics and Ads
  • Meta/Facebook Pixel
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag
  • Hotjar or similar UX tools
  • Mailchimp or similar email platforms
  • Other CRM, marketing automation, or performance tracking software

4. Purpose of Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Track website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve platform functionality
  • Deliver targeted advertising
  • Remember your login or form preferences

5. Cookie Consent

A cookie banner will appear upon first visit to our site. By clicking “Accept,” you consent to our use of cookies. You may choose to reject non-essential cookies.

6. Cookie Duration

Cookies may remain on your device from your session duration up to 2 years, depending on their function and type. Specific durations vary by service provider.

7. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies via:

  • Your browser settings
  • Our cookie preference center
    Note: Disabling some cookies may affect website functionality
